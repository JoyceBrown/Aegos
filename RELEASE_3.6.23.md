# Aegos 3.6.23

## Subscription diagnosis

- Subscription failures distinguish invalid URL, download/network failure, authorization or expiry, unsupported format, unsupported protocol, and empty content.
- Batch updates retain a structured issue for each failed subscription.
- User guidance states whether to retry locally, change subscription format, or contact the provider.

## Acceptance

- Existing URL/format/protocol fixture audits remain required alongside the Stage 6 issue-code gate.
